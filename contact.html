Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Aranya Nature Stay

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website booking system, by calling us directly at +91 98230 99025, or by sending an email to bookings@aranyanaturestay.com. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak seasons.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible cancellation policy. Cancellations made 7 days or more prior to arrival will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 3-6 days of arrival will receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, cancellations made within 48 hours of arrival are non-refundable.

Do you offer pick-up and drop-off services?

Yes, we offer complimentary pick-up and drop-off services from the nearest town (up to 30 km away). For distances beyond 30 km, we charge a nominal fee. Please inform us at least 24 hours in advance if you require this service.

Are children allowed at the resort?

Yes, children of all ages are welcome at Aranya Nature Stay. We have family-friendly accommodations and some activities suitable for children. However, please note that some areas of the property may have natural hazards, and children should be supervised at all times.

What activities are available nearby?

We offer a variety of nature-based activities including guided nature walks, bird watching, stargazing, meditation sessions, and bonfires. Additionally, there are several hiking trails and waterfalls in the surrounding area that our staff can help you explore safely.
